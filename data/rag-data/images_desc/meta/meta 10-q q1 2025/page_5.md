**Significant Trends:**

*   **Total Expenses as % of Revenue:** Showed a significant decline from 75% in Q1'23 to 59% by Q3'23, remaining relatively stable around 58-62% through Q3'24. It hit a low of 52% in Q4'24 before rising to 59% in Q1'25. Overall, a substantial reduction from early 2023 levels.
*   **Cost of Revenue:** Decreased from 21% in Q1'23 to 18% by Q3'23 and has largely stabilized at 18-19% since then.
*   **Research & Development:** Decreased from 33% in Q1'23 to 26% in Q4'23. It then fluctuated, ending at 29% in Q1'25, which is still lower than Q1'23 but higher than its Q4'24 low of 25%.
*   **Marketing & Sales:** Decreased from 11% in Q1'23 to 7-8% by Q3'23 and has largely remained stable at 7% since Q1'24.
*   **General & Administrative:** Exhibited the most volatility, peaking at 13% in Q2'23, then dropping significantly to 6% by Q3'23, and further to a low of 2% in Q4'24. It rebounded to 5% in Q1'25.
