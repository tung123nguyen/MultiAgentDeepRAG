**Significant Trends:**

*   **Overall Growth:** Total advertising revenue shows a strong upward trend from Q1'23 ($28,101M) to Q4'24 ($46,783M).
*   **Seasonal Pattern:** A clear seasonal pattern is evident, with Q4 consistently being the highest revenue quarter each year, followed by a decline in Q1.
*   **Dominant Region:** US & Canada is consistently the largest revenue contributor across all quarters, representing approximately 45-47% of total revenue.
*   **Consistent Growth Across Regions:** All geographic segments show a general growth trend over the observed period.
*   **Q4'24 Peak:** All regions reached their highest revenue figures in Q4'24 (US & Canada: $20,982M; Europe: $11,154M; Asia-Pacific: $9,012M; Rest of World: $5,635M).
*   **Q1'25 Decline:** Following the Q4'24 peak, all regions experienced a sequential decline in Q1'25, consistent with the seasonal pattern.

**Text Summary:**
Revenue by user geography is estimated based on the geographic location of users engaged in revenue-generating activity. This method differs from revenue disaggregation in consolidated financial statements, which is based on customer addresses.
