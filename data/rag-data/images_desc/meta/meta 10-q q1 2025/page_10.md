**Text Summary:**

*   **DAP Definition:** A daily active person (DAP) is a registered and logged-in user of Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, and/or WhatsApp (Family products) who visited at least one of these products via mobile app or browser on a given day.
*   **Exclusions:** DAP numbers do not include users on other products unless they qualify based on other Family product activities.
*   **Methodology:** Calculations attribute multiple user accounts to individual people using complex techniques, algorithms, and machine learning models. Estimates have a margin of error.
*   **Change in Reporting:** Beginning in Q4'23, Family metrics no longer include Messenger Kids users.
