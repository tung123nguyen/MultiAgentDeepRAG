**Text Summary:**
- DAP is defined as a registered and logged-in user of Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, and/or WhatsApp who visited at least one Family product via mobile app or browser on a given day.
- DAP numbers exclude users on other products unless they qualify based on Family product activities.
- DAP calculations use complex algorithms and machine learning to estimate unique people, matching user accounts across multiple products.
- These models are based on internal reviews and calibrated against user survey data.
- In Q3 2022, an update contributed approximately 30 million DAP to worldwide DAP in September 2022.
- Starting Q4 2023, Family metrics no longer include Messenger Kids users.
