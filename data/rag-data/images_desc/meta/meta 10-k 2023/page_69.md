**Summary of Financial Data:**

**Textual Facts:**
*   Worldwide Daily Active Users (DAUs) increased 6% to an average of 2.11 billion in December 2023, up from 2.00 billion in December 2022.
*   Users in India, Bangladesh, and Nigeria were the top three sources of DAU growth in December 2023.
*   Monthly Active Users (MAUs) are defined as registered and logged-in Facebook users who visited Facebook via a website, mobile device, or Messenger application in the last 30 days.
*   As of December 31, 2023, total MAUs were 3.07 billion, an increase of 3% from December 31, 2022.
*   Users in India, Bangladesh, and Nigeria were the top three sources of MAU growth in 2023.
