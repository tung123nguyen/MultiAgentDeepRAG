**Text Summary:**

Facebook's key user metrics (DAUs, MAUs, ARPU) exclude Instagram, WhatsApp, or other products unless they qualify as DAUs or MAUs. User trends directly impact revenue and financial results. Daily Active Users (DAUs) are defined as registered and logged-in Facebook users visiting Facebook via website, Messenger, or Facebook app on a given day, measured as a percentage of MAUs. Europe includes users in Russia and Turkey; Rest of World includes Africa, Latin America, and the Middle East.
