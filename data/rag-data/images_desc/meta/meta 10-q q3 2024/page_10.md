**Text Summary:**
- A Daily Active Person (DAP) is a registered and logged-in user of Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, and/or WhatsApp (Family products) who visited at least one product via a mobile device application or web/mobile browser on a given day.
- DAP numbers do not include users on other products unless they qualify based on Family product activities.
- DAP calculations attribute multiple user accounts across products to individual people using complex techniques, algorithms, and machine learning models. These models are calibrated against user survey data and have a margin of error.
- In Q3 2022, an update to Family metrics calculations contributed approximately 30 million DAP to reported worldwide DAP in September 2022.
- Beginning in Q4 2023, Family metrics no longer include Messenger Kids users.
